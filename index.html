SNOW GOATs

About

The Snow GOAT is an honor bestowed only to the greatest of Snow legends. Martin Beals, current reigning Snow GOAT, combines the strengths of Michael Jordan, Elon Musk, and Bill Murray with none of their flaws.

Realizing that there are many other ServiceNowians that are also awesome like Marty we decided to open it up to everyone. Will you get a piece of acrylic? Probably not. Will you be emblazoned on a static website until we forget to renew the domain? Most definitely.

Do you have what it takes to be the next Snow GOAT? Want to nominate a SNOW GOAT? Submit your nominations at GitHub!
